Off-campus reality (read this first)

  • Nobody hands you an interview — your profile brings it. Keep Resume + LinkedIn + GitHub + LeetCode strong.
  • Referrals > cold applies — connect with people on LinkedIn and ask for referrals (5× more callbacks).
  • DSA is king — online assessments and interviews across every tier rest on it.
  • Your real projects + experience already make you interview-worthy — the gap is DSA consistency.

Full-time daily routine (~8 hours)

  • ~4h — DSA: learn a pattern + solve LeetCode problems (the core)
  • ~1.5h — CS fundamentals + LLD / System design
  • ~1h — early: profile & projects · later: applying + referrals
  • ~1h — Aptitude (for service exams) + revision
  • ~0.5h — mock / review · 1 rest day/week

DSA — how to practise

  • Learn patterns, not random problems — two pointers, sliding window, binary search, trees, graphs, DP.
  • Follow one sheet: Striver A2Z / SDE Sheet or NeetCode 150.
  • Solve Easy → Medium (mediums are where offers are won); add Hards for FAANG.
  • Stuck 20–30 min? Read the approach, then re-solve from scratch next day.
  • Target: ~150–250 for product, ~300–400 for FAANG.

Retention (so you don't forget)

  • Active recall — re-solve problems without looking; don't just re-read solutions.
  • Spaced repetition — revisit a topic after 1 day → 1 week → 1 month.
  • Maintain a notes/mistake log — patterns, edge cases, silly errors.
  • Feynman — explain a solution aloud (or to AI); gaps show instantly.

Projects & development (your edge)

  • Keep your best 2–3 projects polished with live links + clean READMEs.
  • Be ready to explain every decision & trade-off (why this DB, how auth works, scaling).
  • Stay sharp in your stack (JS/TS, Angular/React, Node/Laravel, SQL/NoSQL, Git, Docker).

Applying strategy

  • Portals: LinkedIn, Naukri, Instahyre, Wellfound, Cutshort + company career pages.
  • Service exams: TCS NQT, Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant, Accenture, Capgemini (register on their portals).
  • Apply daily once basics are done; chase referrals; track every application.

Resources

  • DSA: Striver (takeUforward), NeetCode, LeetCode, GeeksforGeeks
  • System Design / LLD: Striver LLD, Grokking the System Design
  • Aptitude: IndiaBix / R.S. Aggarwal · Mock interviews: Pramp / peers

Do's & Don'ts

  • ✅ Solve DSA daily · learn patterns · polish projects · apply + chase referrals · do mock interviews.
  • ❌ Don't hoard 10 resources · don't only watch (solve yourself) · don't wait till "fully ready" to apply · don't skip aptitude for service roles.
